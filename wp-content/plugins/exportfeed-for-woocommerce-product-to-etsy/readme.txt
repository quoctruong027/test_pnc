== Installation ==

>[**HOW TO GUIDE**](http://www.exportfeed.com/tos) |||
>[** ALL FAQS **](http://www.exportfeed.com/faq/)
<P>
A few quick steps<P>
1) Upload to your WordPress website, and activate 'WooCommerce Products to Etsy'.<BR>
2) Select ‘Etsy Feed' from admin menu, Click 'Create Feed'<BR>
3) Follow the pop up to setup your etsy account.<BR>
4) Once its done, search your product using your desired filter.<BR>
5) Assign the corresponding Etsy category to each products and click 'Move Selected'.<BR>
6) (Optionally) Name your feed.<BR>
7) Goto 'Product' page from admin menu and start uploading your product to etsy shop.<BR>
7) See the uploaded detailes in Listing/Upload submenu of Etsy Feed admin menu.<BR>

<P>Click --Get Feed--
<P>
This will now redirect to the feed URL you just created - ready for submission!

== Screenshots ==
1. Provide Etsy Keystring and Secret Key and click on Save and Continue & Connect
2. Click on Fetch Login URL to start connection with Etsy
3. Click on Connect to Authenticate to provide app permissions
4. Click on Allow Permissions on the Etsy permissions page
5. Click on Authorize to allow permissions for app to manage your feeds
6. Click on Show My Shop to continue setting up your Etsy Settings
7. Create new shipping or select your existing shipping rules pulled from Etsy
8. Select your WooCommerce category & corresponding Etsy category. Then give name for feed file.
9. Click on Create Feed button then click on View Feed button
10. Go to Manage Feed and hover over the feed to click on the Upload link
