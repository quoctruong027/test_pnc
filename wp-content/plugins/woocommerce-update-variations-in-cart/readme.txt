Allows your customers to update product variations in the cart.

== Description ==

WooCommerce Update Variations In Cart is an amazing extension which allows your customer to change variation in cart itself. They can opt for different color or size of a T-shirt while they are in checkout process.

If you don't have this extension and your customer wants to purchase different variant of a product, then he/she will have to first remove that product from cart & then again will have to visit that product's page & will have to perform process of selecting desired variant & adding it to cart again. This process is quite hectic & might be boring also for your customer and will ask more efforts from your customer.

So, WooCommerce Update Variations In Cart, is here to make your customer's life a lot easier. Add this great add-on to your WooCommerce store & give your customer 'power to change variation in cart'.

It's a powerful add-on for your WooCommerce powered store, as it allows your customer to change variations of multiple products in cart on click of a button.

== Highlights ==

* Easier in installation, no additional settings, just install and activate.
* Automatically enable 'update variation' to all existing or new product variations
* Powerful add-on as it allows changing variations of multiple products in cart with just one click.
* Makes your customer's purchasing experiance easier.

== Installation ==

1. Ensure you have latest version of [WooCommerce](https://wordpress.org/plugins/woocommerce/) plugin installed
2. Unzip and upload the folder 'woocommerce-update-variations-in-cart' to your `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
3. Activate 'WooCommerce Update Variations In Cart' through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= What are the requirement to use this extension? =

This extension will work with any website powered with Wordpress 3.3+ & WooCommerce 2.5+

= Does this work only with WooCommerce? =

Yes, this add-on will only work with WooCommerce 2.5+

= Does it allows updating multiple variations in cart? =

Yes, it allows changing multiple variations in cart on click of a button
